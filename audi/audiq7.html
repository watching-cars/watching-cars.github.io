Moving dynamics

The standard quattro all-wheel drive ensures sporty handling. With the Audi drive select dynamic
handling system, which also comes as standard, you can actively influence the vehicle’s
characteristics, including the Adaptive Air suspension that regulates the vehicle’s height. All
engines come with mild-hybrid technology as standard, which ensures efficient driving resulting in
reduced consumption.

Audi Q7 Variants: It is available in two trims: Premium Plus and Technology.

Audi Q7 Seating Capacity: Audi’s flagship SUV is a 7-seater.

Audi Q7 Engine and Transmission: It is powered by a 3-litre turbo-petrol engine (340PS/500Nm), paired with an 8-speed
automatic. The new Q7 continues with Audi’s all-wheel drivetrain

Audi Q7 Features: Features on board include a panoramic sunroof, four-zone climate control, a digital driver’s display, and a
10.1-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Android Auto and Apple CarPlay.

Audi Q7 Safety: Audi has equipped its flagship three-row SUV with lane departure warning, front and rear parking sensors,
park assist, and electronic stability control.
